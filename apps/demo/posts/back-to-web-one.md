The web used to be a place you owned. You had a site, it was yours, and
everything you wrote lived at your address. Then we handed it all to a
handful of platforms.

This post is the first experiment in taking it back: it lives on my own
site, but it's also a record in the open AT Protocol network. The comments
below are real Bluesky replies to it.
